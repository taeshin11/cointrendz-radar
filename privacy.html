Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

CoinTrendz Radar collects minimal, anonymous data to improve the service:

  • Page visit data: Timestamp, page URL, browser user agent, browser language, and timezone.
  • Local storage data: Theme preference, language preference, and visitor count (stored on your device only).

We do not collect names, email addresses, IP addresses, or any personally identifiable information (PII).

2. How We Use Data

Anonymous visit data is used solely to:

  • Understand traffic patterns and popular features
  • Improve the user experience
  • Monitor service health

3. Cookies

CoinTrendz Radar itself does not set cookies. However, third-party services may set cookies:

  • Google AdSense: May use cookies for ad personalization. You can opt out at Google Ad Settings.
  • Adsterra: May use cookies for ad delivery and analytics.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • CoinGecko API: For cryptocurrency data (no user data shared)
  • Alternative.me API: For Fear & Greed Index (no user data shared)
  • Google AdSense: For advertising
  • Adsterra: For advertising
  • Google Sheets: For anonymous visit logging

5. Data Storage

Visit data is stored in Google Sheets and is retained for analytics purposes. Local preferences (theme, language) are stored in your browser's localStorage and never leave your device.

6. Children's Privacy

CoinTrendz Radar is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. GDPR Compliance

We do not collect or store personal data. All analytics data is anonymous. If you have concerns, contact us at taeshinkim11@gmail.com.
