About CoinTrendz Radar

What Is CoinTrendz Radar?

CoinTrendz Radar is a free, real-time cryptocurrency sentiment and market data dashboard designed to give investors, traders, and enthusiasts an instant read on the pulse of the crypto market. At a glance, you can see the current Fear & Greed Index, the day's top-gaining and top-losing coins, live prices for Bitcoin and Ethereum, and the coins that are trending across the CoinGecko platform right now. The dashboard refreshes automatically every 60 seconds so your data is never stale.

Whether you are an active day trader looking for an edge, a long-term investor checking whether the market is overheated or presenting a buying opportunity, or simply someone curious about what is happening in crypto today, CoinTrendz Radar delivers the essential signals you need without the noise. No account required, no paywalls, and no complicated interfaces — just clear, actionable data presented beautifully on any device.

Why Market Sentiment Matters for Crypto Investors

The cryptocurrency market is notoriously driven by emotion. Unlike traditional financial markets, crypto prices can swing dramatically in hours based on social media narratives, news events, and collective investor psychology. Understanding market sentiment — the overall attitude of investors toward an asset — is therefore one of the most powerful tools available to crypto participants of any experience level.

The legendary investor Warren Buffett famously advised to "be fearful when others are greedy and greedy when others are fearful." This principle applies with particular force in crypto, where sentiment swings are amplified. When the market is gripped by extreme fear, historically many assets have traded well below their fair value, presenting potential long-term entry opportunities. Conversely, periods of extreme greed have often preceded sharp corrections, signaling that it may be time for caution.

CoinTrendz Radar makes this sentiment data immediately accessible. By checking the Fear & Greed Index daily, investors can develop a disciplined habit of incorporating market psychology into their decision-making process rather than reacting impulsively to price movements.

Key Features Explained in Detail

Fear & Greed Index Gauge

The centerpiece of CoinTrendz Radar is the live Fear & Greed Index gauge. Powered by data from Alternative.me, this index condenses a complex range of market signals into a single number from 0 to 100. The score is displayed on an intuitive dial that ranges from deep red (Extreme Fear) through amber (Neutral) to bright green (Extreme Greed). The current sentiment label — Extreme Fear, Fear, Neutral, Greed, or Extreme Greed — is displayed beneath the gauge so the meaning is clear at once.

Bitcoin & Ethereum Live Price Cards

The two flagship cryptocurrencies, Bitcoin (BTC) and Ethereum (ETH), each have a dedicated live price card. Each card displays the current price in USD, the 24-hour percentage change, market capitalization, and 24-hour trading volume. A sparkline chart visualizes the price movement over the past 24 hours, allowing you to see at a glance whether the price has been rising, falling, or moving sideways — without needing to open a separate charting platform.

Top Gainers & Top Losers Tables

CoinTrendz Radar tracks the top 250 cryptocurrencies by market capitalization and identifies the ten coins that have gained the most and the ten coins that have fallen the most in the past 24 hours. These tables are updated in real time and include each coin's rank, name, ticker symbol, current price, and 24-hour percentage change. This feature is invaluable for spotting breakout moves early and for understanding where momentum is flowing in the market right now.

Trending Coins

The Trending Coins section highlights the coins that are receiving the most search attention on CoinGecko in the past 24 hours. Search trends are a powerful leading indicator — coins that people are searching for often attract buying interest shortly after. This section lets you stay ahead of the curve and discover emerging narratives before they reach mainstream media coverage.

Dark Mode & Multi-Language Support

CoinTrendz Radar is designed for global use. The interface is available in eight languages — English, Korean, Japanese, Chinese, Spanish, French, German, and Portuguese — with automatic detection of your browser's language on first visit. A dark mode toggle lets you switch between light and dark themes, with your preference saved locally in your browser so the setting persists across sessions.

How the Data Is Sourced

All cryptocurrency price data, market capitalization, trading volume, and trending coin data is sourced directly from the CoinGecko API, one of the world's most respected and widely-used cryptocurrency data aggregators. CoinGecko aggregates data from hundreds of exchanges to deliver accurate, comprehensive market information.

The Fear & Greed Index is sourced from Alternative.me, which calculates the index daily using five weighted data inputs: market volatility (25%), market momentum and volume (25%), social media sentiment (15%), surveys (15%), and Bitcoin dominance (10%). The index has been running since 2018 and is one of the most cited sentiment indicators in the crypto space.

CoinTrendz Radar does not manipulate, adjust, or editorialize the data it receives. What you see is a direct, clean representation of real market data. We make no representations about the future performance of any cryptocurrency based on this data.
