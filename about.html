About CoinTrendz Radar

What is CoinTrendz Radar?

CoinTrendz Radar is a free, real-time cryptocurrency dashboard that provides at-a-glance market sentiment analysis. We display the Fear & Greed Index, today's top gaining and losing coins, and trending search coins -- all updated automatically every 60 seconds.

Who is it for?

  • Crypto traders and investors (beginner to intermediate)
  • Market watchers who want quick sentiment snapshots
  • Content creators looking for trending coin data
  • Anyone curious about cryptocurrency market conditions

Data Sources

All cryptocurrency data is sourced from CoinGecko, one of the most trusted crypto data aggregators. The Fear & Greed Index comes from Alternative.me. We do not provide financial advice -- all data is for informational purposes only.

Features

  • Fear & Greed Index gauge with real-time updates
  • Bitcoin and Ethereum price cards with 24-hour sparkline charts
  • Top 10 gaining coins by 24-hour percentage change
  • Top 10 losing coins by 24-hour percentage change
  • Trending coins from CoinGecko search data
  • Auto-refresh every 60 seconds
  • Dark and light mode themes
  • Multi-language support (8 languages)
  • Mobile-responsive design
