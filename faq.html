Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Fear & Greed Index?

The Fear & Greed Index measures market sentiment on a scale of 0 to 100. Values near 0 indicate extreme fear (potential buying opportunity), while values near 100 indicate extreme greed (potential market correction). The index is calculated from factors including volatility, market momentum, social media, and surveys.

How often does the data update?

The dashboard auto-refreshes every 60 seconds, pulling the latest data from CoinGecko and Alternative.me APIs. You can see the countdown timer in the top bar.

Is CoinTrendz Radar free?

Yes, CoinTrendz Radar is completely free to use. No account or subscription is required.

Where does the data come from?

Cryptocurrency prices, market data, and trending coin data are sourced from the CoinGecko API. The Fear & Greed Index is sourced from Alternative.me.

How are "Top Gainers" and "Top Losers" determined?

We fetch the top 100 coins by market cap from CoinGecko and sort them by their 24-hour percentage price change. The top 10 positive changes are "gainers" and the top 10 negative changes are "losers."

What are "Trending Coins"?

Trending coins are the most searched coins on CoinGecko in the last 24 hours. This reflects what the crypto community is currently interested in.

Is this financial advice?

No. CoinTrendz Radar provides data for informational purposes only. Always do your own research (DYOR) and consult a qualified financial advisor before making investment decisions.

Can I change the language?

Yes. Use the language selector in the top-right corner of the page. We support English, Korean, Japanese, Chinese, Spanish, French, German, and Portuguese.
